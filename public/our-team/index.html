Our Team

Joan Dao, Business Supervisor

Joan is an American tech entrepreneur with a diverse background in fashion, tech, and bio-science industries. She currently serves as the CEO of Ilesovi Inc, a company that specializes in creating high-end security IoT fashion devices. Joan is a firm believer in the power of tech-powered ingenuity, and she is living proof that innovation and creativity come from collaboration of seemingly unrelated interests. She holds a Bachelor's of Science degree in Neuroscience from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

Vanessa Gallimore, Community Outreach & Distribution

Vanessa is a Jamaican service and hospitality specialist with a special interest in empowering her community with access to modern technology. She has partnered with Jamaican companies in industries ranging from food service to international tourism. As a mother of two young children, Vanessa understands the importance of equipping future generations with the tools they need to succeed. She graduated with her Bachelor's in Hospitality Services from the Culloden Vocational Training Center in Whitehouse, Jamaica.

Joan Puteri Zheng, Founder & Tech Lead

She currently holds a research position at a tech firm in Minneapolis, MN, and has multiple publications in the field of computer science and natural language processing. She is passionate about youth advocacy and technology outreach programs.
